Brain Tumor Detection Using Deep Learning

"Smart AI for Smarter Diagnosis-Brain Tumor Classification Made Easy."

Our project aims to leverage advanced machine learning techniques to assist in the early detection of brain tumors through MRI scans. By providing a user-friendly interface, we hope to aid healthcare professionals in making timely and accurate diagnoses, ultimately improving patient outcomes.
